The daily journey

A day in AltLeads — Agent

1

Log in & see your work

crm.altleads.com → email + password. Dashboard shows what's pending; sidebar has Leads, Companies, Contacts, Meetings, Wish List, Approvals.

2

Open your call-list

Go to Contacts or Companies, pick your project — the list scopes to your work.

3

Call or email

Open the record → one click to Call or Email. (click-to-call is part of the upcoming UX polish.)

4

Log what happened

Set the call outcome (Connected / No Answer / Interested…), the status (Hot / Warm / Cold…), and a note. It's added to the Activity timeline instantly.

5

Move it forward

Schedule a meeting or submit for approval — the right people get an email + in-app alert automatically. Then on to the next contact.

What a record holds

Everything about a prospect, in one place

🏢 Company

  • Name, website, industry, city, CIN, owner
  • Per-project Account Status, Feasibility, Decision Power
  • Its Contacts & its Leads
  • Full Activity history

👤 Contact

  • Name, designation, company (masked details)
  • Per-project Status, description, comments
  • Log-a-Call panel
  • Associated Leads & Colleagues

🎯 Lead

  • Full workspace: info + Activity / Report / Meeting tabs
  • Stage progress & approvals
  • Linked to its company & contact

HubSpot-style associations

Company ↔ Contacts ↔ Leads ↔ Meetings are all linked — open one and reach the rest. Create a lead from inside a contact or company, pre-filled.

Privacy by design

Masking — protect the contact details

Everyone can see

  • Contact name
  • Company & city
  • Notes & status

Only owner + manager + admin can see

  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • LinkedIn

No confusion

When a detail is restricted, the system shows it as locked (not just blank) — so people know it exists and who owns it. Enforced by the database, per project — not just hidden in the screen.

Hand-offs & oversight

Nobody misses a hand-off

🔔 Notifications

Automatic email + in-app alerts on: lead assigned/reassigned, meeting scheduled/rescheduled/cancelled, approval requested/approved/sent-back. Each email links into the CRM.

👔 Manager (Team Lead)

Sees the team's pipeline. Approves or sends back lead reports with feedback. Reassigns work — the new owner is notified.

🛠️ Admin

Adds users & logins, manages pick-lists/projects/access, and maintains data in bulk — keeping 500+ companies current without burdening the team.

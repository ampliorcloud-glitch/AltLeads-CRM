The Superpower

Capture everything now → an AI advantage later

"All call logs between 11am–2pm are unanswered, but after 3pm always picked — even small details are captured. Pick a contact and it gives me suggestions, action items, and content to reach out with."

How it works

  • Every call, email & note is logged with time, outcome & context.
  • That data is organised and embedded on the go.
  • An AI retrieves it instantly (RAG) to answer questions and suggest the next move.

What it unlocks

  • Per-contact: best time to call, what worked before, tailored talking points.
  • Per-month: "100 companies whose contracts renew next month — reach out now."
  • Insight no competitor has, because only we recorded it.

⚠️ The one thing we must get right from day one

The AI is only as good as the data we capture. Rich, structured logging of every interaction has to start at launch — it can't be back-filled later. This is the single most important "can't change later" decision.

⚠️ Risk to clear before go-live — the ownership/write-path

Today a person can edit a record only if they created it. But our data was bulk-imported, so the "creator" isn't the assigned agent. Unfixed, an agent gets "you can only edit records you own" on their whole call-list. Fix: agents edit records assigned to them (a focused change), validated with a real agent login before launch. This is a business decision (how is "assigned" defined?) more than an engineering one.
